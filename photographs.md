They have been selected because they show different aspects of Sudanese history, including the beginnings of Khartoum University, activities of Sufi orders, prominent politicians, musicians, and athletes, and daily life from the 1950s and 60s. In the process, they illustrate the diversity of Sudanese life in the period of early independence.
